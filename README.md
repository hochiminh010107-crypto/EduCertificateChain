# EduCertificate Chain

## Project Title

EduCertificate Chain

## Project Description

EduCertificate Chain is a decentralized academic credential registry built on Soroban and the Stellar blockchain. The platform enables universities, schools, training centers, and certification authorities to issue tamper-proof digital certificates that are permanently recorded on-chain.

Students can own and manage their academic credentials through blockchain wallet addresses, while employers, institutions, and third parties can instantly verify certificate authenticity without relying on centralized databases.

By leveraging decentralized technology, EduCertificate Chain reduces credential fraud, simplifies verification processes, and establishes a trusted ecosystem for educational and professional achievements.

## Project Vision

The vision of EduCertificate Chain is to create a global decentralized infrastructure for educational credentials. Through Stellar and Soroban smart contracts, the platform provides secure, transparent, and immutable academic records that remain accessible throughout a learner's lifetime.

The project aims to bridge the trust gap between educational institutions, students, employers, and certification providers by enabling instant credential verification anywhere in the world.

## Key Features

* **Institution Registry:** Administrators can authorize and manage educational institutions and certificate issuers.
* **Certificate Issuance:** Authorized issuers can create blockchain-based academic and professional credentials.
* **Student Ownership:** Certificates are linked to student wallet addresses for secure ownership and identification.
* **Certificate Categories:** Support multiple credential types such as Degrees, Diplomas, Course Completions, Professional Licenses, and Achievement Badges.
* **Certificate Verification:** Anyone can verify certificate authenticity using a unique certificate identifier.
* **Certificate Revocation:** Issuers can revoke certificates that are invalid, fraudulent, or issued in error.
* **Expiration Support:** Credentials can optionally contain expiration dates for licenses and certifications.
* **Student Credential Index:** Students can maintain multiple credentials under a single blockchain identity.
* **Immutable Records:** All credential information is stored permanently on-chain.
* **Transparent Validation:** Public verification ensures trust between institutions, students, and employers.
* **Event Logging:** Certificate issuance, revocation, and issuer management activities are recorded through blockchain events.

## Usage Instructions

1. **Initialize Contract:** Deploy the smart contract and assign a system administrator.
2. **Register Issuer:** The administrator authorizes educational institutions or certification providers.
3. **Issue Certificate:** An authorized issuer creates a certificate for a student wallet address.
4. **Store Credential:** Certificate metadata and verification information are permanently stored on-chain.
5. **Retrieve Credentials:** Students can view all certificates associated with their wallet address.
6. **Verify Certificate:** Employers, universities, and organizations can verify credential authenticity using the certificate ID.
7. **Revoke Certificate:** Authorized issuers may revoke certificates when necessary.
8. **Query Records:** Anyone can access certificate information and verification status for transparency.

## Why Blockchain?

Traditional credential systems often suffer from document forgery, slow verification processes, centralized databases, and high administrative costs.

EduCertificate Chain addresses these challenges by providing:

* Immutable credential storage.
* Instant verification without intermediaries.
* Reduced risk of certificate fraud.
* Permanent accessibility of academic records.
* Transparent and auditable credential management.

## Future Scope

* **Soulbound Certificates:** Issue non-transferable blockchain credentials tied permanently to student identities.
* **IPFS Integration:** Store certificate files on IPFS while maintaining verification hashes on-chain.
* **QR Code Verification:** Enable instant credential validation through QR codes.
* **Multi-Institution Ecosystem:** Support universities, schools, and certification providers worldwide.
* **Academic Transcript Support:** Manage complete educational histories and learning achievements.
* **Employer Verification Portal:** Build dedicated verification dashboards for recruiters and organizations.
* **Analytics Dashboard:** Track issuance statistics, verification requests, and institutional performance.
* **Cross-Platform Credential Sharing:** Allow interoperability with educational and employment platforms.

## Technology Stack

* Rust for smart contract development.
* Soroban SDK for Stellar smart contracts.
* Stellar blockchain for decentralized credential management.
* Cryptographic hashing for secure certificate verification.
* On-chain storage for immutable academic records.
